When you log in to Toggl Track, you can't miss the big pink start button. It's the perfect way to track what you're doing right now. 

You can also continue an existing time entry by hovering and clicking on the start button that appears next to time you have already tracked.

And you can use manual mode to log time if, like me, you forgot to start the timer Just click on the icon of 3 lines below the clock, enter the day, start time, and stop time for your time entry, and click the pink check mark.

So many options!
